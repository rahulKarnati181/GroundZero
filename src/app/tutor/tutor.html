FAQ for Tutors

How are payouts calculated and when are they processed?

Payouts are per session. Your dashboard shows the live tally. We settle to your bank account on a monthly cycle within the first 5 working days; mid-month advances are available for sustained load.

What are the expected weekly hours?

Most tutors start with 4–10 hours per week. You set availability; we match sessions into those slots. Ramp up or down anytime with notice.

Can I teach only weekends?

Yes. Weekend-only schedules are supported. We’ll route weekend demand to your open slots.

What if a student cancels?

With 12+ hours’ notice, the session is rescheduled. Late cancellations count toward payout as per policy; no-shows are fully paid.

Do I get materials for different boards?

Yes. We provide board-aligned practice sets and concept sheets (CBSE/ICSE/IGCSE/IB/State). You can bring your own resources too.

What does the short interaction cover?

A 15-minute chat on your approach, comfort grades, availability, and basic policies. It’s not a test—just alignment before matching.
