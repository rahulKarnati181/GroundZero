Where It All Began

GroundZero started with a simple promise: put great teachers back at the center of learning.

Built by a community of teachers with 10+ years in real classrooms, GroundZero Learnings helps students build concepts that last — not just answers for the next test.

After helping more than 10,000 students across India and abroad, we found one truth: every child learns differently. We begin by understanding how a student thinks, then design a plan to help them learn faster, smarter, and with confidence.

We realised homework doubts don’t wait for class time, so we built 24/7 doubt solving into the default plan, not a premium add-on.

Why I started GroundZero

Classrooms were crowded, feedback was slow, and students needed a guide who could map how they think. GroundZero began as a home table with two students and one promise: teach the child, not the chapter.

  • 1:1 first Every plan is designed for one child. No batch logic.
  • Board-aligned CBSE, ICSE, IGCSE, IB, State — same concepts, cleaner paths.
  • Visible progress Short loops, checkpoints, and parent updates that show the change.
